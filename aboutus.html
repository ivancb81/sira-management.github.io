About Us

We Are SIRA
SIRA Management is Indonesia’s integrated business improvement partner, providing solutions in sales and leadership, productivity, and management systems to enable sustainable growth for organizations. Founded in 2023, SIRA is supported by partners with expertise and backgrounds from national and multinational companies across various industries. We focus on helping people develop the right mindsets, methods, and best practices to deliver measurable results.

Our Purpose

To leave a legacy that makes a positive impact at every crucial moment for individuals, organizations, and nations.

Our Mission

"To provide practical and impactful solutions in people development, process optimization, and system enhancement for organizations, built on a foundation of safety and sustainability.."

Our Vision

"To be the trusted business transformation partner— a greenhouse for sustainable growth and success."

About The Vendor

SIRA Management - Sales & Leadership Practitioner
Ludo Siswanto is a Sales and Leadership Practitioner with over 18 years of experience in various strategic roles in sales, team leadership, and professional development. He began his career as an automotive sales executive and later as a Relationship Manager at a human capital development organization, where he consistently achieved 100% of his sales targets for two consecutive years.  

He later advanced to the role of Sales Performance Product Manager as well as Trainer & Consultant, contributing to the implementation of sales performance improvement programs based on international methodologies. He has also delivered leadership training for professionals and managers, supporting their capability development across diverse industries, including finance, manufacturing, distribution, beauty and aesthetics, startups, plantations, and textiles.

Ludo further enhanced his expertise through professional development at a Sales Academy program from the United States held in Singapore, focusing on building consultative selling competencies.  

His capabilities were strengthened by earning certifications as an ISO 9001:2015 Lead Auditor (IRCA) and a Lean Six Sigma Green Belt while working at a multinational company specializing in inspection, verification, and certification services. In his previous role as Academy Manager and business unit leader, he led a multidisciplinary team responsible for training operations, business development, and client engagement. During this period, Ludo successfully maintained 100% annual target achievement, with average training revenue growth of 25–30% per year over three consecutive years.  

Currently, Ludo serves as the Managing Director of SIRA Management and is recognized as a Sales Leadership Expert who helps organizations transform their people, processes, and performance to achieve sustainable business growth.
